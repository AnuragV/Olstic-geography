How to Play Wordlock

  • Look at the letters on the grid and figure out what word they spell
  • Drag to create a path connecting letters in the correct word order
  • Move only UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT (no diagonal movement)
  • Your path must visit ALL cells on the grid (like Zip)
  • Easy: 5x5 grid, 5-letter words
  • Medium: 6x6 grid, 6-8 letter words
  • Hard: 7x7 grid, 8-10 letter words
