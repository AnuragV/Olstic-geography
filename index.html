How to play

Allocate weight across 8 categories. Each question has base value 10 points. Your chosen weight changes the points per question as explained below. You can use each leverage once during the quiz (0.5×, 2×, 3×) to change points on a question.

  • 6.25% → 5 points per correct (−5 for wrong)
  • 12.5% → 10 points per correct (−10 for wrong)
  • 18.75% → 15 points per correct (−15 for wrong)
  • 25% → 20 points per correct (−20 for wrong)

Drag sliders to set each category's weight. The values snap to 4 discrete positions: 6.25, 12.5, 18.75 and 25. Total must equal 100.
